Web design

Designing for the web

Good design is not only about making things look good, but also about delivering a good user experience (ux).Having a great colour palette matters little if your content does not deliver your message. Therefore we believe in designing engaging user experiences along with great content. In recent years, there has been a strong focus on building responsive websites, that allow users to view their favourite websites on any of their devices in a scaled version. There are some great benefits to this approach:

  1. Usability

    All of your content will adapt based on the device you are using, thus making it easier for the user of the website, as they do not have to zoom to read content. Images will resize according to the size of the device you are using.

  2. Maintainability

    If you have a website that scales down according to your device, you only really need one website, making it easier for you to maintain, and easier to scale in the future.

Search engine optimization (SEO)

Having great content is one part of every great website, however, if google or yahoo does not show your website in the search results, who's going to read your great content? Search engine optimisation is about having your website rank high in what is referred to as the 'organic search results'. This term covers the searches that are done by real life human beings, and are not generated by clicking on advertisement adds such as google AdWords or Microsoft Advertising.
